What is included

Records cover companies, research groups, facilities, infrastructure, workforce programs, public initiatives, operating environments, and generalized defense assets with documented relevance to unmanned or autonomous systems.

Evidence standard

Every published record includes at least one public source and an explanation of its unmanned-systems relevance. Source-backed records remain clearly marked as pending until a publisher completes editorial review.

Location precision

Airport reference points may use published coordinates. Most organizations, programs, and security-sensitive assets use generalized campus or locality coordinates. A map point does not represent permission to operate an unmanned system.
